Welcome to Bracketing!

Our website was created for the final project for SE 186X at Iowa State University.


Team Members: Mitchell Talyat, Ben Dubin, Nick Thomas, & Emily Huisinga


Similar to March Madness, our website allows you to vote on brackets about a variety of different topics. In each bracket, you will be presented with pictures or gifs representing different items from the chosen topic. You will be able to select which items you want to move on in the bracket, ultimately deciding your final bracket.

All of your decisions for your bracket will be recorded and points will be assigned to each item depending on how far you advance it in the bracket. After completing a bracket, you will be able to see a ranked list of how each item in the bracket has done across everyone's brackets.


Mascots Bracket

This bracket allows you to rank mascots from different universities throughout the Midwest.
